Application Fields:

(1) Multi-Mode Intelligent Cleaning

The 3WWDZF6-50DS supports various flight modes, including manual, automatic, altitude hold, and point targeting. These features enable precise positioning and stable hovering, allowing the drone to easily tackle complex cleaning tasks on building facades.

(2) High Load Capacity

This cleaning drone has a maximum payload capacity of 80 kg, which supports extended continuous cleaning operations. Under standard load conditions, the battery provides an impressive endurance of up to 50 minutes, meeting the high-efficiency cleaning demands of large areas.

(3) Efficient Flight Performance

The 3WWDZF6-50DS exhibits exceptional flight performance, with a maximum ascent speed that can reach 5 m/s. Additionally, the horizontal flight speed can achieve up to 20 m/s in attitude mode, ensuring rapid coverage of the cleaning area and significantly enhancing operational efficiency.

(4) Precise Hovering and Positioning

Equipped with a dual-satellite positioning system that includes both GPS and BeiDou, the drone achieves a hovering accuracy of up to 0.5 meters. This feature ensures that the drone can accurately align itself with cleaning targets, even in complex environments.

(5) Adaptability to Harsh Environments

The 3WWDZF6-50DS boasts an IP56 protection rating, which signifies its robust dust and water-resistant design. This allows the drone to operate effectively under various weather conditions, ensuring that it can perform cleaning tasks successfully, even in light rain.

(6) Convenient Operation and Quick Deployment

The drone can be conveniently controlled through a ground station app or a remote controller, allowing for easy remote operation. Additionally, its design supports quick folding, enabling transport and deployment in less than 5 minutes, which significantly enhances on-site operational efficiency.
