Black Light Camera

Designed for all-weather surveillance, excelling in night and low-light conditions with advanced AI-ISP (Artificial Intelligence Image Signal Processor) technology, switching modes automatically between day and night to ensure optimal image quality and energy efficiency.

  • Day Mode

    Traditional ISP technology: During daytime, employing conventional image signal processing techniques to ensure image quality while maintaining power consumption equivalent to traditional AOV (visual analytics and optimization) devices.

  • Night Mode

    AI-ISP technology: At night, utilizing AI-ISP technology to slightly increase power consumption while reducing the energy consumption associated with constant illumination from fill lights.

    Full-color night vision: Using AI technology to filter out irrelevant data, achieving full-color images, and enhancing object recognition.

    Energy-saving effect: Significantly reducing the activation time of infrared lights, resulting in substantial power savings.

Mid-Far Infrared Night Vision Device

Using advanced mid-far infrared technology to provide long-distance high-precision night vision in extremely low-light conditions.
